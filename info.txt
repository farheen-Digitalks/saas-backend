
===============================
SAAS BACKEND PROJECT FLOW
===============================

PROJECT TYPE:
-------------
Multi-Tenant SaaS Backend Architecture

TECH STACK:
-----------
- Node.js
- Express.js
- MongoDB
- JWT Authentication
- RBAC (Role Based Access Control)

====================================================
MULTI-TENANT SAAS FLOW
====================================================

PLATFORM USER:
--------------
Internal SaaS users.

Examples:
- Super Admin
- Sales Team
- Support Team

Can manage:
- All companies
- Subscriptions
- Payments
- Products
- Trials

----------------------------------------------------

COMPANY USER:
-------------
Belongs to specific company only.

Examples:
- HR
- Employee
- Manager
- Accountant

Can access:
Only their company data.

====================================================
ROLE & PERMISSION FLOW
====================================================

Platform creates permissions
        ↓
Company creates roles
        ↓
Permissions assigned to roles
        ↓
Users assigned roles

Example:

Role:
- HR Manager

Permissions:
- CREATE_EMPLOYEE
- UPDATE_EMPLOYEE
- VIEW_EMPLOYEE

====================================================
SUBSCRIPTION FLOW

        ↓

Payment verification
        ↓
Subscription activated
        ↓
Modules enabled
